Kirk Lee Hammett

American guitarist, songwriter, vocalist. Best known as a member of the band "Faster rock". He was a pupil of the guitar virtuoso Joe Satriani. He owns a series of ESP guitars (models KH). In addition to them uses Gibson Les Paul, Jackson Randy Rhoads, ESP Flying V and guitar company Fender. As a child, Kirk showed great interest in his brother's music collection, which significantly influenced his decision to engage in guitar. At the age of 15 he bought his first electric guitar for money earned in Burger King. Hammett grew up under the influence of bands like Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, Deep Purple, Status Quo, The Rolling Stones, Jimi Hendrix, UFO. The greatest influence on his work was rendered by Jimi Hendrix.
